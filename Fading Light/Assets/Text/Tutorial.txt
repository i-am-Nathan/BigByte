PB::Welcome to Fading Light! Get through traps and puzzles to escape the cave!
PB::Pressing TAB shows and hides the controls for the game.
PB::Each player has its own abilities. The younger sibling can fit into small spaces however she cannot push heavy boxes.
PB::The older sibling however has more health than the younger sibling and can push heavy boxes.
PB::All objects with a green glow are interactable by pressing the interact keys (O and Q) when close by. These objects include levers, post boards, treasure chests and wall torches.
PB::Some objects can also be picked up throughout the game. These include extra lives, gold, health potions, attack potions and a defense potion.
PB::Attack potions and defense potions last for 20 seconds. Good luck.
